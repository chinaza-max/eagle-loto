FAQs

Who can play Eagle Lot games? icon

Eagle Loto is open to all Nigerians up to the age of 18.

How do I win a game? icon

To win a game, you first enter the draw by giving the right answer to the game question. At the end of the game, our system selects the first 30 players with correct answers as the winners.

Can I play more than one game in a day? icon

Yes you surly can. There are no limits to the number of games you can play on Eagle Loto.

Can I win multiple games? icon

There are no winning limits.

How do I buy game coins? icon

You can buy game coins from within the app, using the Deposit menu. You are able to purchase coin using your Card or through bank transfer.

How do I withdraw my winnings? icon

You can withdraw your game coins / winnings to your bank account at anytime.
